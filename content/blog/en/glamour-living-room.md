A glamour style living room is synonymous with elegance, luxury, and refined taste. This unique interior design style combines rich materials, shiny surfaces, and sophisticated details, creating a space full of shine and chic. In this article, we'll show you how to arrange a **glamour style living room**, whether you live in a spacious house or an apartment.

## Characteristic Features of Glamour Style

Glamour style is characterized primarily by:

- **Shiny surfaces**: mirrors, glass, glossy metals such as silver or gold.
- **Luxurious materials**: velvet, silk, satin, fur.
- **Wealth of details**: crystal chandeliers, decorative frames, decorative pillows.
- **Subdued color scheme**: whites, blacks, grays, beiges dominate, often broken by accents in intense colors like purple or navy.

## How to Arrange a Glamour Style Living Room?

![Glamour style living room](/blog/salon-glamour-1.webp)

### Furniture Selection

Choose furniture with elegant lines and high-quality finishes. A sofa upholstered in velvet in a deep color such as emerald or navy will be the ideal central point of the living room. Coffee tables with marble tops and metal legs will add shine and luxury.

### Lighting

Lighting plays a key role in glamour style. A crystal chandelier hung above the relaxation zone will add elegance to the interior. Complete the arrangement with table and floor lamps with decorative bases and lampshades in light colors.

### Accessories and Decorations

Don't forget about details that will emphasize the character of the interior. Mirrors in decorative frames will optically enlarge the space and add shine. Pillows with shiny appliqués, soft throws, and rugs with long pile will introduce coziness.

## Glamour Living Room in an Apartment – Is It Possible?

Of course! Even in a small apartment, you can create a glamour style interior. The key is skillful use of space and selection of appropriate decor elements. In small rooms, it's worth choosing light wall colors that will optically enlarge the space, while shiny surfaces and mirrors will add depth.
