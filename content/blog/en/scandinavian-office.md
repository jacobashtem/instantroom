## Scandinavian Style Office – How to Create a Functional and Cozy Workspace

More and more people are working remotely, which is why proper home office arrangement is becoming extremely important. **Scandinavian style office** is an excellent solution for those who value minimalism, functionality, and a cozy atmosphere conducive to concentration. In this article, we'll present inspiration and tips on how to arrange a **Scandinavian style office**, combining aesthetics with ergonomics.

### Characteristic Features of Scandinavian Style in the Office

- **Light colors**: whites, grays, and beiges dominate, optically enlarging the space and promoting well-being.
- **Natural materials**: wood, linen, cotton give the interior warmth and authenticity.
- **Minimalism**: simple forms, no unnecessary decorations, focus on functionality.
- **Ergonomics**: furniture and solutions promoting comfortable work.

![Scandinavian style office](/blog/styl-skandynawski-biuro-1.webp)

### Choosing Furniture for a Scandinavian Office

- **Desk**: simple, made of natural wood or white, with minimalist design. It's important that it has adequate work surface and is adapted to the user's needs.
- **Chair**: ergonomic, ensuring comfort during long working hours. It can be upholstered in light colors or with wooden elements.
- **Shelves and bookcases**: open shelves made of natural wood allow storage of necessary items and add lightness to the interior.
- **Dressers and cabinets**: closed furniture helps maintain order and hide documents and office equipment.

### Scandinavian Style Office Arrangement

#### Colors and Materials

- **Walls**: best painted white or in light shades of gray, promoting concentration and not distracting attention.
- **Floors**: wooden or panels in light colors. They can be complemented with a rug made of natural fibers for greater comfort.
- **Accessories**: subdued, made of natural materials such as wood, ceramics, or fabrics.


#### Lighting

- **Natural light**: if possible, place the desk near a window to benefit from as much daylight as possible.
- **Desk lamps**: simple, functional, with adjustable arm, allowing light direction adjustment.
- **General lighting**: ceiling lamps or ceiling lights with minimalist design, ensuring even lighting throughout the room.
![Scandinavian style office arrangement](/blog/styl-skandynawski-biuro-2.webp)

#### Accessories and Decorations

- **Plants**: green accents introduce freshness and improve air quality, which is important in the workplace.
- **Inspiration boards**: cork or magnetic, allowing you to pin notes, inspiration, or plans.
- **Art**: minimalist graphics, posters with nature motifs, or motivational quotes.

### Ergonomics and Space Organization

- **Ergonomic setup**: monitor at eye level, keyboard and mouse within reach, appropriate desk and chair height.
- **Order**: use desk organizers, boxes, and baskets to keep the space clean and avoid distraction.
- **Work zones**: if space allows, separate a work zone and a relaxation zone, e.g., with a comfortable reading chair.
