## Scandinavian Style Bathroom – How to Create a Cozy and Functional Space

The bathroom is a place of relaxation and regeneration, so its arrangement should promote calmness and comfort. **Scandinavian style bathroom** is an excellent solution for people who value simplicity, natural materials, and functionality. In this article, we'll show you how to arrange a Scandinavian style bathroom, paying attention to characteristic features, materials, colors, and ideas for **small bathrooms with a shower and washing machine**.

### Characteristic Features of a Scandinavian Style Bathroom

- **Light colors**: whites, grays, beiges dominate, optically enlarging the space and introducing calm.
- **Natural materials**: wood, stone, ceramics add warmth and authenticity to the interior.
- **Minimalism**: simple forms, no unnecessary ornaments, functionality first.
- **Functionality**: thoughtful solutions facilitating daily use.


## Materials and Colors in a Scandinavian Bathroom

### Colors

- **White**: the basic color, symbolizes cleanliness and optically enlarges space.
- **Grays and beiges**: introduce warmth and harmony.
- **Color accents**: delicate pastel shades of blue, green, or pink can appear in accessories.

### Materials

- **Wood**: used on floors, furniture, accessories; best protected against moisture.
- **Stone and ceramics**: natural tiles on walls and floors.
- **Glass**: shower cabins, shelves, accessories.

## Equipment and Furniture

### Sink and Cabinet

- **Minimalist forms**: countertop or built-in sinks.
- **Natural wood cabinets**: simple, functional, with plenty of storage space.

### Bathtub or Shower?

- **Freestanding bathtub**: if space allows, adds elegance.
- **Walk-in shower**: with transparent glass doors, optically enlarges the room.

## Small Scandinavian Style Bathroom with Shower and Washing Machine

Arranging a **small bathroom with a shower and washing machine** in Scandinavian style is a challenge, but with thoughtful solutions you can create a functional and aesthetic interior.

![Scandinavian style bathroom](/blog/styl-skandynawski-lazienka-1.webp)

### Arrangement Tips

- **Use of light colors**: optically enlarges space.
- **Built-in washing machine**: hidden in a cabinet or under a countertop, doesn't disturb the interior aesthetics.
- **Walk-in shower**: takes up less space than a traditional cabin, facilitates arrangement.
- **Custom furniture**: allows maximum use of available space.

## Accessories and Lighting

### Lighting

- **Natural light**: if possible, it's worth using a window in the bathroom.
- **Artificial lighting**: ceiling lamps, wall sconces with simple forms; warm light, promoting relaxation.

### Accessories

- **Plants**: moisture-resistant, introduce freshness.
- **Textiles**: towels, rugs in subdued colors, made of natural materials.
- **Accessories**: containers, baskets made of wood or wicker, maintained in minimalist style.
