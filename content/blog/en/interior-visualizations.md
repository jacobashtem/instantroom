Today we present **Instant Room** – the first Polish AI tool that allows for instant creation of interior visualizations based on your photos. Without complicated programs, without long hours spent searching for inspiration – now you have everything at your fingertips.

## How does it work?

Using Instant Room is extremely simple and intuitive:

### 1. Upload a photo of your room

It can be a living room, bedroom, kitchen, or even a terrace. If you don't have your own photo, you can use our library.

### 2. Choose the type of room

Our tool supports various types of rooms:

- Living room
- Dining room
- Children's room
- Terrace
- Bathroom
- Kitchen
- Bedroom
- Office

### 3. Choose the arrangement style

You have **30 styles** to choose from, including:

- **Modern**

- **Rustic**
- **Boho**
- **Industrial**
- **Minimalist**
- **Glamour**
- **Japandi**
- **Vintage**
- **Eclectic**
- **And many others!**

### 4. Done!

In a few seconds, you'll receive a visualization of your room in the chosen style. You can experiment with different options until you find the perfect one.

## Frequently asked questions

### What photos to upload to get the best result?

It's best to use well-lit photos taken from an angle that covers as much of the room as possible.

### Can I use visualizations commercially?

Yes! Your visualizations have no watermark and you can use them for both personal and commercial purposes.

### How long does visualization generation take?

It usually takes a few seconds. Depending on server load, it may take slightly longer.
